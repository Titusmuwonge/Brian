Our Programs

Comprehensive solutions to transform lives in Africa

Education Programs

Education is the foundation for breaking the cycle of poverty. Our schools provide quality education to orphans and vulnerable children, with specialized programs for children with disabilities.

Our Primary Schools

Serving over 300 students with quality primary education, including specialized classes for children with disabilities.

Training Institutes

Provides marketable skills in tailoring, carpentry, knitting, and more for youth who cannot afford traditional schooling.

Scholarship Program

Supports secondary and university education for qualifying students to pursue higher education.

Medical Care Programs

Access to healthcare is critical for the well-being of children and families in our community. Our medical programs address both immediate health needs and long-term wellness.

Medical Clinics

Provides general medical care, HIV/AIDS treatment, maternal health services, and health education to over 500 patients monthly.

HIV/AIDS Treatment Program

Comprehensive care including antiretroviral therapy, counseling, and nutritional support for HIV+ individuals.

Emergency Medical Fund

Provides financial assistance for critical medical needs including surgeries, cancer treatment, and congenital conditions.

Children's Home

A safe, loving environment for orphans and vulnerable children who have no other family to care for them.

Residential Care

Provides housing, nutrition, healthcare, and emotional support for children ages 2 months to 17 years.

Family-Style Living

Children live in small groups with dedicated caregivers to create a family environment.

Holistic Development

Focuses on spiritual, emotional, physical, and educational growth for each child.

Sustainability Programs

Long-term solutions that create self-sufficiency for individuals and the organization.

Our Farms

Extensive farm land producing food for our programs while teaching sustainable agriculture techniques.

Income-Generating Projects

Includes vocational graduate businesses, farm produce sales, and craft production to support our programs.

Community Development

Partners with local families to improve economic stability through microloans and skills training.

Changing families from dependency to self-sustainability in Africa.
