Our Programs

Comprehensive solutions to transform lives in Africa

Our Impactful Initiatives

We focus on long-term, sustainable solutions that empower communities and create a ripple effect of change.

Education

From providing scholarships to building new schools, we are committed to giving every child the opportunity to learn.

Agriculture & Sustainability

We train local farmers in modern techniques and manage a self-sustaining farm that feeds our students and families.

Medical Care

Our medical teams provide free healthcare, preventative education, and access to treatment for vulnerable communities.

Our Comprehensive Approach

We believe in holistic change. Our programs are interconnected, addressing the core needs of a community to build a foundation for a prosperous future.

Education & Vocational Training

Empowering children and young adults with essential skills for their future.

AEaT Farm

25-acre farm producing food for our programs while teaching sustainable agriculture techniques.

Income-Generating Projects

Includes vocational graduate businesses, farm produce sales, and craft production to support our programs.

Community Development

Partners with local families to improve economic stability through microloans and skills training.

American Education & Teachers
