Who We Are

American Education and Teachers (AEaT) is a faith-based non-profit organization dedicated to empowering orphans and vulnerable families.

Our organization is rooted in the community it serves. We work hand-in-hand with local leaders to deliver comprehensive programs in education, medical care, and economic sustainability.

Our Core Values

Faith

Our work is guided by faith, hope, and compassion for all people.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and accountability to our donors and the communities we serve.

Empowerment

We provide tools and training to empower individuals toward self-sufficiency.

Leadership

The team dedicated to our mission

American Education and Teachers is guided by dedicated leaders in both Africa and the USA. Together, they ensure accountability, transparency, and long-term sustainability in every initiative.

Their vision is rooted in faith, compassion, and a firm belief that communities can rise when given the right tools, resources, and opportunities.
