Our Mission

Empowering communities to thrive through education and sustainability.

Who We Are

American Education and Teachers (AEaT) is a faith-based non-profit organization operating in both the USA and Uganda. Our mission is to alleviate poverty and foster self-sufficiency by empowering families with the tools and resources they need to create a brighter future.

We focus on providing holistic support that addresses the root causes of poverty, including education, healthcare, and economic development. Our model is rooted in local partnership, ensuring that our programs are community-led and sustainable for generations to come.

Leadership & Governance

American Education and Teachers is guided by dedicated leaders in both Uganda and the USA. Together, they ensure accountability, transparency, and long-term sustainability in every initiative.

Their vision is rooted in faith, compassion, and a firm belief that communities can rise when given the right tools, resources, and opportunities.
