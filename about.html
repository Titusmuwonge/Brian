About Us

Our story, our mission, and our commitment to a better future.

Our Story

Founded in 2012 by Pastor Charles and his wife Esther Mugisha in Kampala, Uganda, American Education and Teachers began with a simple vision: to bring hope to a nation struggling with poverty, illiteracy, and disease. Starting with a small number of vulnerable children, the organization has grown to support thousands of families through education, medical care, and sustainable development.

Leadership & Vision

American Education and Teachers is guided by dedicated leaders in both Uganda and the USA. Together, they ensure accountability, transparency, and long-term sustainability in every initiative.

Their vision is rooted in faith, compassion, and a firm belief that communities can rise when given the right tools, resources, and opportunities.

Be Part of the Story

Your support allows us to continue transforming lives. Whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word — you are bringing hope to families in Uganda.
