Our Programs

We offer comprehensive solutions to transform lives in Africa.

Education

We provide quality education to children and vocational skills to adults, empowering a new generation.

Healthcare

We offer essential medical care, health education, and support to families in remote villages.

Sustainability

We implement sustainable projects, like farms and micro-loans, to help communities become self-reliant.

Changing lives from dependency to self-sustainability

AEaT is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities in Uganda, Africa. We believe that true transformation comes from providing the tools and knowledge necessary for long-term self-sufficiency.

Through our comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, and economic development, we work alongside local leaders and families to create lasting change that echoes for generations.
